# The Heat Index

The Heat Index represents a golf rankings website that highlights the top 5 hottest players on the PGA Tour and LPGA Tour based on current-season performance. Instead of relying on traditional world rankings that include a complex algorithmn difficult to decipher based on results from previous years, this project uses a simple formula based on wins, top 10 finishes, majors won, and cuts missed to show who is playing the best right now. The site includes a homepage with current rankings, an About page explaining the formula and the advantage of using this system, and a Past Rankings page that tracks previous weeks to reflect changes as the season progresses.

## Purpose

As a golf enthusiast over the years, I have always wondered why the golf rankings are so incredibly complex and do not always reflect an accurate depiction of the current golf landscape. I built this project to make golf rankings easier to understand for everyday fans like myself. Traditional systems like the OWGR and Rolex Rankings reward long-term consistency over a snapshot of 2-3 years, but they can be slow to reflect who is actually in form during the current season. This project is for golf fans who want a simpler and more current way to compare players, spot who is hot at the moment, and make more informed decisions based on recent performance.
