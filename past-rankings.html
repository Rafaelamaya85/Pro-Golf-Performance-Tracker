Past Rankings

This page tracks previous weekly rankings for both the PGA Tour and LPGA Tour so users can see how player performance changes over time. It is important because it shows movement in the rankings, highlights who is staying hot, and gives more context than just looking at one week's list.

Example: Previous Week Rankings

  • 1. Scottie Scheffler
  • 2. Rory McIlroy
  • 3. Wyndham Clark
  • 4. Xander Schauffele
  • 5. Collin Morikawa

Why This Page Matters

Past rankings help users compare current results with earlier weeks and better understand momentum, consistency, and overall season performance. Instead of only seeing who is ranked highest today, visitors can also follow trends and changes throughout the season.
