🔥 The Heat Index

About the Project

The Heat Index was created to make golf rankings easier to understand for everyday sports fans. Traditional rankings in golf can be confusing, slow to change, and difficult for casual viewers to follow. This project focuses on current-season performance to highlight which players are actually playing the best right now.

This ranking system is needed because other golf rankings do not always clearly show who the hottest players are in the current season. The Heat Index is designed to be simpler and more transparent by rewarding strong results that fans can easily understand.

  • Wins are rewarded the most.
  • Top 10 finishes show consistency.
  • Missed cuts lower a player’s score.
  • Major wins receive bonus points.

The goal of this website is to give users a more straightforward way to compare PGA and LPGA players, track changes over time, and better understand who is performing at the highest level each week.
